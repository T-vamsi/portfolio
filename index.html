About Me

Hello, I'm Vamsi Turaga

I am an aspiring Data Analyst and Data Science enthusiast with a strong interest in transforming raw data into actionable insights. Coming from a hands-on learning background, I enjoy working on real-world problems where data can drive smarter decisions and improve outcomes.

I have experience working with tools like Python, SQL, and Excel for data analysis, along with visualization tools such as Power BI. I am also exploring machine learning concepts and continuously building my skills in areas like data cleaning, data extraction, and automation. My work often involves practical problem-solving—whether it's extracting data from websites, analyzing patterns, or creating efficient workflows using tools like Selenium.

In addition to data analytics, I have a growing foundation in full-stack development, which helps me understand how data systems integrate with real-world applications. This allows me to think beyond just analysis and focus on how insights can be implemented effectively.

I am passionate about learning, building, and improving every day. My goal is to become a well-rounded data professional who can not only analyze data but also create impactful solutions that solve business problems.
